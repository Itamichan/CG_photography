FAQ

What is the price for a photo session?

The price varies per category, as well as per duration and number of photographs. Please contact me for more details.

Are photo sessions taking place only in Stockholm?

No. You can choose any location within Stockholm county. For specific remote locations or another country please contact me directly.

Can we have a photo session multiple days in a row?

Yes! It is important to notify me well in advance to make this happen.

What is the duration of a photo session?

The minimum duration of a photo session is one hour. An average photo shoot takes around four hours but we can agree on longer periods.

How many photographs will we get?

The included number is 20 photographs for couples photography and 50 photographs for wedding. Additional photographs can be agreed with extra charge.

Will the photographs be edited?

Yes. Of all photographs taken you can choose the agreed upon number which will be professionally edited.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. With every photo shoot you will get a 5% discount voucher which you can use or give to a friend.

Can we have different clothing styles during a photo session?

Yes. This is highly recommended. More clothing, more styles, more fun.
